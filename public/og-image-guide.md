# Open Graph Image Guide

## Create Your OG Image

### Specifications
- **Size:** 1200 x 630 pixels
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Max File Size:** 8MB (recommended < 1MB)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 1.91:1

### Design Tips
1. **Include Logo** - Place your logo prominently
2. **Add Text** - Keep it short and readable
3. **Use Brand Colors** - Match your website theme
4. **High Contrast** - Ensure text is readable
5. **Test on Mobile** - Preview on different devices

### Tools to Create OG Image
- [Canva](https://www.canva.com/) - Free templates
- [Figma](https://www.figma.com/) - Design tool
- [Adobe Express](https://www.adobe.com/express/) - Quick designs
- [OG Image Generator](https://og-image.vercel.app/) - Automated
