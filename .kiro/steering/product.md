# Product Overview

**Gerobak Jogja** is a business website for a cart manufacturing company based in Yogyakarta, Indonesia. The company specializes in creating and selling various types of carts (gerobak) for food vendors and small businesses.

## Core Features

- **Product Catalog** - Showcase different cart types with detailed specifications
- **Image Gallery** - Visual portfolio of completed projects and cart designs
- **WhatsApp Integration** - Direct ordering through WhatsApp for Indonesian market
- **Admin Panel** - Content management system for products, gallery, blog, testimonials, and FAQs
- **Blog System** - Content marketing with Markdown support
- **Contact Forms** - Customer inquiries with Firebase storage

## Target Audience

- Food vendors and small business owners in Indonesia
- Entrepreneurs looking for mobile cart solutions
- Existing customers seeking additional carts or modifications

## Business Model

- Custom cart manufacturing and sales
- Direct customer communication via WhatsApp
- Portfolio-driven marketing through gallery and testimonials

## Key Differentiators

- Local Indonesian market focus (Bahasa Indonesia content)
- WhatsApp-first customer communication
- Visual-heavy presentation with professional gallery
- Admin-managed content for easy updates
